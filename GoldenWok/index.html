OUR STORY

Golden Wok Chinese restaurant offers delicious dining, takeout and delivery in Westbury, New York. Our restaurant is a cornerstone in the Westbury community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. Our restaurant is also known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
